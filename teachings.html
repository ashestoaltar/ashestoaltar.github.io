Foundations Series

A twelve-lesson journey through Scripture as a unified covenant story—rooted in Torah, fulfilled in Messiah, and lived out in the power of the Spirit.

Foundations · Lesson 1

What the Bible Actually Is

The Bible as a unified covenant story—creation, calling, exile, and restoration— not a random anthology, but the testimony of God and His people fulfilled in Messiah.

How Scripture teaches through narrative, patterns, and typology—repeated shapes like exodus, exile, remnant, and kingdom—rather than abstract systems or proof-texts.
