Privacy Policy

This site is a simple static website hosted on GitHub Pages and served through the domain ashestoaltar.com. No custom tracking scripts, analytics pixels, or advertising networks are intentionally used here.

However, like most websites, basic technical information may be collected automatically by the hosting platform (GitHub Pages) and by your browser or network. This can include things such as:

  • IP address and approximate region
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring website or page
  • Date and time of access

This information is generally used in aggregate to keep the site running securely and to prevent abuse. I do not sell or share this information for marketing purposes.

This site links out to other platforms (for example, Medium, YouTube, and X). Those services have their own privacy policies and data practices. Please review those policies directly on their respective sites.

If you contact me by email, your message and email address will be stored in my personal inbox so that I can respond. I will not share that correspondence publicly unless you explicitly ask me to.

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, an updated version will be posted on this page.

If you have questions or concerns about anything written here, you can reach me at contact@ashestoaltar.com.
